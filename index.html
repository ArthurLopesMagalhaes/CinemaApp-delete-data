Account Deletion Request Process:

We respect your privacy and offer a straightforward process for account deletion. To request the deletion of your account, follow these steps: 

        Sent an email to arthurlopescontact@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request".
        In the email body, include your full name and the email address associated with your account.
        Our support team will process your request and send you a confirmation email once your account has been permanently deleted.
        Please note that account deletion is irreversible, and your data will be permanently removed from our servers.
Types of Data Deleted: Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:
  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Usage history and app activity
  • Custom preferences and settings
